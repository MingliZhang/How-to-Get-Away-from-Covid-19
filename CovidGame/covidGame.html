Covid Protection Methods Game

We will introduce you to five steps of how to protect yourself from Covid 19
This game is similar to 2048, and you can use four direction keys to play this game. You will always start with the basic step "wash your hand at least 20s" and you may want to combine the same step to get a new protection method.
You will win the game and protect yourself after you generate all 5 protection approaches.

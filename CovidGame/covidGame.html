Covid Protection Game

step
0

How to play: This game is similar to 2048, and you can use four direction keys to play this game. You will always start with the basic step "wash your hand at least 20s" and you want to combine the same step to get a new protection method. You will win the game and protect yourself after you generate all 5 protection approaches.

 Wash your hands carefully! Check our hand wash timer here!

 Clean & disinfect all surfaces using swipes! Coronavirus could live on smooth surfaces for ~2days.

 Stay 6 feet away from others to prevent yourself from potential virus carriers. Remember, one could be a virus carrier even if he does not have a symptom!

 Even if you do not show symptoms, wear a mask to protect both you and others from spreading of virus and further reduce the risk of being infected.

 If you show symptoms, contact your health provider immediately and schedule Covid tests! Quarantine yourself until the symptom goes away.

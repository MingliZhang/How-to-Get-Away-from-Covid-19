Statistical Facts

Globally, we have over 39M people infected. Everyone could be under the risk of Covid-19 if proper protections are not utilized, but people over 60s with underlying medical problems should be extra careful since they are at higher risk of developing serious illness.

How to protect yourself

Wash your hands, wear masks, and wait 6 feet from others! If possible, you should also use other cleaning supplies such as hand sanitizers and disposable gloves.

Common symptoms of Covid

Most importantly usually includes fever and cough. Also could lead to pneumonia, acute respiratory distress syndrome, sepsis, septic shock, and kidney failure.

All possible symptoms
If I had some symptoms...

Stay at home and contact your health care provider to schedule a Covid-19 test immediately. Even if the test result is negative, you should continue to stay at home for at least 14 days until the symptom disappears.
